ABOUT US

Hetman Equipment Group, Construction Parts, Drilling & Mining Supplies

WHO WE ARE

Hetman Equipment Group has merged with Bismark Equipment to provide Construction Parts, Drilling & and Mining Supplies. John Ataman founded Bismark Equipment in 1990 with the ambition to offer quality products great customer service with the lowest possible prices. Hetman Equipment Group specializes in constuction parts and supply a full range of percussion bits, shanks, rods, drill pipe, and accessories both O.E.M. and after market. We provide replacement parts & drills plus Screen Pushers. We supply parts for Air Trac, Caterpillar, Furukawa, Gardner -Denver, Ingersoll-Rand, Case, Hitachi and Terex. We offer Carbide Cross Bits, Button Bits, Steel Bits in a variety of sizes including 41, 43, 45, 48, 51. Drill steel sizes include T32, T38, T45, T48, T51, We offer services for both Mining and the Constuction Industry.

At Hetman Equipment Group we strive for great customer service. We go that extra mile to do whatever we can to make our customers feel that they are in good and capable hands. 30 years of experience and still going strong!
